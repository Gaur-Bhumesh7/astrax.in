ABOUT US

AstraX is the annual astro-meet of IIT Mandi, the biggest astronomical meet in India. Bringing together Astronomy experts and curious amateurs and everyone in between. Combining space science and technology, AstraX is the hub of astronomy enthusiasts, hosting a plethora, competitive and nail-biting events and proficient talks with prominent speakers in their fields. It is the center of attraction for students and professors with an inclination towards computational and theoretical astronomy. AstraX brings to you a cosmic sea of worth of knowledge and astronomical fun, all wrapped up in a four day long meet.
